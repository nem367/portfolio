Consciously, Green Shopping Guide

Consciously is a mobile application that works as a green shopping guide for customers and retailers. After scanning bar code of products, users can find inforgraphics on ESG facts about a product and brand as well as a comparison with other products.
Tools & skills used: Figma, Canva, Google Forms, Coolors, Adobe Colors, Usability Testing, User Interviews
